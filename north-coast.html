KENYA BEACHES

North Coast Beaches

Discover pristine white sands, coral reefs, and vibrant coastal towns.

Overview

Kenya’s North Coast is famous for its stunning beaches such as Nyali, Bamburi, Shanzu, Watamu, and Malindi. The region offers crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and rich Swahili culture.

Visitors enjoy snorkeling, diving, dhow sailing, and relaxing beach holidays along the warm Indian Ocean coastline.

It is the perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural exploration.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Mombasa North Coast
  • Best Time: December - March
  • Famous For: Coral Reefs
  • Activities: Snorkeling & Beach Holidays
  • Experience: Luxury & Culture
