Amboseli National Park

Discover vast elephant herds with breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa’s highest peak.

Overview

Amboseli National Park is one of Kenya’s most iconic safari destinations, famous for its large elephant herds and dramatic backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro. The park’s open plains and swamps attract a wide variety of wildlife including lions, giraffes, zebras, cheetahs, and over 400 bird species.

It is one of the best places in Africa to get close-up views of elephants in the wild, often walking in front of the snow-capped mountain, creating one of the most photographed safari scenes in the world.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Kenya
  • Best Time: June - October & January - February
  • Famous For: Elephants & Kilimanjaro views
  • Activity: Game Drives & Photography
