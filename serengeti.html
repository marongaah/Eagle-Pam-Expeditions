Serengeti National Park

Witness the Great Migration and experience one of Africa's greatest wildlife spectacles.

Overview

Serengeti National Park is one of Africa’s most iconic safari destinations, renowned for vast plains teeming with wildlife.

The park hosts the Great Migration, where millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles move across the ecosystem in search of fresh grazing.

Visitors can also encounter lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, giraffes, and countless bird species throughout the year.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Northern Tanzania
  • Best Time: June - October
  • Famous For: Great Migration
  • Wildlife: Big Five
  • Activity: Game Drives
