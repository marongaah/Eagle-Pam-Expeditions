TANZANIA SAFARI

Tarangire National Park

Discover ancient baobab trees, vast savannahs, and some of Africa's largest elephant herds.

Overview

Tarangire National Park is one of Tanzania’s most underrated safari destinations, known for its scenic landscapes and abundant wildlife.

The park is especially famous for its large elephant populations and iconic baobab trees that dominate the landscape. During the dry season, wildlife gathers around the Tarangire River, creating exceptional game-viewing opportunities.

Visitors can encounter lions, leopards, cheetahs, giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, and over 500 bird species.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Northern Tanzania
  • Best Time: June - October
  • Famous For: Elephant Herds
  • Wildlife: Predators & Elephants
  • Activity: Game Drives
