KENYA BEACHES

South Coast Beaches

Relax in the world-famous Diani Beach and explore Kenya's most beautiful coastline.

Overview

The South Coast is home to some of Kenya’s most famous beaches including Diani Beach, Tiwi Beach, and Msambweni.

It is known for soft white sands, coral reefs, and luxury beachfront resorts.

Activities include kite surfing, diving, snorkeling, camel rides, and dhow cruises.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Kwale County
  • Best Time: December - March
  • Famous For: Diani Beach
  • Activities: Water Sports
  • Experience: Luxury Beach Resorts
