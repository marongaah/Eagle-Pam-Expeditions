KENYA SAFARI

Tsavo West National Park

Discover dramatic volcanic landscapes, crystal-clear springs, and abundant wildlife in one of Kenya's most scenic safari destinations.

Overview

Tsavo West National Park is one of Kenya's most diverse safari destinations, characterized by volcanic hills, lava flows, acacia woodlands, and rich wildlife habitats. Together with Tsavo East, it forms one of the largest protected wilderness areas in Africa.

The park is renowned for Mzima Springs, where millions of litres of crystal-clear water emerge daily from underground volcanic aquifers. The springs attract hippos, crocodiles, fish, and a variety of bird species.

Visitors can encounter elephants, lions, leopards, buffaloes, giraffes, zebras, and many other wildlife species while enjoying spectacular landscapes and breathtaking viewpoints throughout the park.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Southeastern Kenya
  • Best Time: June - October
  • Famous For: Mzima Springs
  • Wildlife: Big Five & Birdlife
  • Activity: Game Drives & Photography
