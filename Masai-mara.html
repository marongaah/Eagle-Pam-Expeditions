KENYA SAFARI

Masai Mara National Reserve

Experience the world-famous Great Migration and Africa’s Big Five in one of the most iconic safari destinations on Earth.

Overview

The Masai Mara National Reserve is Kenya’s most famous wildlife reserve, known for its vast savannahs, rich wildlife, and the annual Great Migration. It is one of the best places in Africa to see the Big Five in their natural habitat, often within a single game drive. Between July and October, millions of wildebeest and zebras cross from the Serengeti into the Mara, creating one of the world’s most dramatic wildlife spectacles. Visitors can also enjoy game drives, hot air balloon safaris, and cultural visits to Maasai communities.
