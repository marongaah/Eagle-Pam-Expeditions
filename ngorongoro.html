Ngorongoro Crater

Explore one of Africa's most remarkable wildlife destinations and the world's largest intact volcanic caldera.

Overview

Ngorongoro Crater is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Africa’s most spectacular natural wonders. Formed by a collapsed volcano millions of years ago, the crater provides a unique ecosystem that supports an incredible concentration of wildlife.

The crater floor is home to lions, elephants, buffaloes, leopards, rhinos, hyenas, zebras, wildebeest, and numerous bird species. It offers some of the best wildlife viewing opportunities in East Africa.

With breathtaking scenery and year-round wildlife encounters, Ngorongoro is a must-visit destination on any Tanzania safari.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Northern Tanzania
  • Best Time: Year-round
  • Famous For: Ngorongoro Crater
  • Wildlife: Big Five
  • Activity: Game Drives
